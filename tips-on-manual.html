Learn Some Manual

Sinuhe's Basic Steps

Hello I am Sinuhe! Hopefully you can learn too!

Here are some tips on learning manual.

Get familiar



Tip 1

Make sure you are on neutral which is usually centered and a bit loose



Tip 2

Make sure you Ebrake is on when in neutral



Tip 3

To go, release the Ebrake and place your foot on the breaks



Tip 4

Press the clutch as far back as it can go, move the shift knob to first gear while still manitaining your foot on the breaks



Tip 5

Slowly release the clutch as if you are in slow motion. While releasing the clutch, evenally match the pressure with the gas peddle



Tip 6

Press the clutch back all the way in to stop. While the clutch is in, move the shift knob back to neutral Put the Ebrake back up. Release the clutch.
