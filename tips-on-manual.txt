Make sure you are on neutral which is usually centered and a bit loose
Make sure you Ebrake is on when in neutral
To go, release the Ebrake and place your foot on the breaks
Press the clutch as far back as it can go 
Move the shift knob to first gear while still manitaining your foot on the breaks
Release the breaks 
Slowly release the clutch as if you are in slow motion
While releasing the clutch, evenally match the pressure with the gas peddle 
Press the clutch back all the way in to stop
While the clutch is in, move the shift knob back to neutral
Put the Ebrake back up
